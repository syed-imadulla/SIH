About The Project

Problem Statement ID: 25048

Build a gamified digital platform to enhance learning outcomes for students in rural schools (grades 6–12), focusing on STEM. The platform should use interactive games, multilingual content, and offline access to engage students with limited internet connectivity.

Expected Outcome

  • Increase student engagement by ~15%
  • Simple analytics dashboard for teachers
  • Deployable on low-cost devices

Technical Feasibility

  • HTML5/CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript
  • Service workers for offline functionality
  • Lightweight and compatible with low-end browsers

Why This Matters

Rural schools often face challenges with internet connectivity and access to engaging educational resources. Our platform addresses these issues by providing offline-first, gamified learning experiences that work even on low-cost devices.

Education Equality

Bridging the educational gap between urban and rural students through technology.

Key Features

Gamified Learning

Points, badges, and levels to motivate students and make learning fun and engaging.

Offline-First

Content is available without internet connection after initial download, perfect for areas with poor connectivity.

Multilingual Support

Content available in English and local languages to cater to diverse student needs.

Teacher Dashboard

Simple analytics to track student progress and engagement with easy content upload.

Lightweight Design

Runs smoothly on low-cost devices and basic browsers, optimized for performance.

Reward System

Badges and certificates to celebrate student achievements and milestones.
